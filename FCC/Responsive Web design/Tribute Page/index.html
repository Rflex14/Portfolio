What is Dark Souls?

Dark Souls is an action JRPG videogame franchise developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco, this series has been criticly aclaimed, to the point where multiple game journalist sources have labeled the first Dark Souls game as one of the Best VideoGames Of All Time

How is it diferent to other action JRPG's?
  • Dark Souls is considerable more difficult than other Action JRPGS of it's time, trusting in the players ability to adapt and overcome challenges.
  • Dark Souls has an unconventional narration method, using it's level design and item descriptions as a way to communicate with the player about the world the game takes place in.
  • Dark Souls has different movesets for each distinct weapon class usable in the game.
  • Dark Souls implements a currency called Souls not only to buy items, but to level up the player character.
  • This Soul currency is dropped on death and can be recovered in the same place the player died unless the player dies again before recollecting them.
  • Dark Souls checkpoints are represented by bonfires spread around the world, in which the player can rest, allowing it to level up, turn human, recover it's health, spell casts, healing charges and respawning enemies.
What does it mean for it's fans?

For some, it's a hard game they proudly have beaten; For others, It's their preferred dark fantasy game where they can become stronger and end up slaying the most abominable beast imaginable, and finally, some people consider it a familiar place as a result of replaying it after beating it the first time, the game's atmosphere is like a warm embrace, welcoming you back to a rich world full of legendary stories, characters and adversaries. Pushing you towards challenging yourself, but allowing you multiple moments of respite.

In a way, this incredibly difficult and sometimes stressful game became a safe haven for people to take a break from their real life problems, breath, and then go back out and triumph.
